Explore Northern Pakistan

Northern Pakistan is home to majestic mountains, vibrant valleys, and diverse cultures. Select from our curated list of destinations to plan your next adventure.

Gilgit-Baltistan

Hunza Valley

Summary: Known as “Heaven on Earth,” Hunza boasts breathtaking peaks and apricot blossoms.

Major Attractions: Altit Fort, Baltit Fort, Attabad Lake.

Best Months: April – October

Culture & Food: Rich Wakhi culture, apricot-based dishes, local Hunza bread.

Travel Cost: PKR 20,000 – 35,000 (5-day trip)

Skardu

Summary: Gateway to K2 with serene lakes and surreal landscapes.

Major Attractions: Shangrila Resort, Satpara Lake, Deosai Plains.

Best Months: May – September

Culture & Food: Balti traditions, “Mamtu” dumplings, butter tea.

Travel Cost: PKR 25,000 – 40,000 (5-day trip)

Fairy Meadows

Summary: Alpine meadows offering the best view of Nanga Parbat.

Major Attractions: Nanga Parbat Base Camp, lush trekking routes.

Best Months: June – September

Culture & Food: Local mountain huts, desi meals served by villagers.

Travel Cost: PKR 15,000 – 25,000 (3-day trip)

Khaplu

Summary: Known as the “Shangri-La of Baltistan” with rich history and scenic beauty.

Major Attractions: Khaplu Palace, Chaqchan Mosque, Hushe Valley.

Best Months: May – September

Culture & Food: Tibetan-influenced food, buckwheat bread, apricot oil cuisine.

Travel Cost: PKR 18,000 – 30,000 (4-day trip)

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KPK)

Swat Valley

Summary: Known as the “Switzerland of the East,” with rivers and lush valleys.

Major Attractions: White Palace, Swat Museum, Fizagat Park.

Best Months: March – October

Culture & Food: Pashtun hospitality, Chapli Kebabs, Lamb Karahi.

Travel Cost: PKR 15,000 – 25,000 (4-day trip)

Kalam

Summary: A serene valley with glaciers and alpine meadows.

Major Attractions: Ushu Forest, Mahodand Lake, Matiltan Waterfall.

Best Months: May – September

Culture & Food: Trout fish, Chapli Kebab, local bread.

Travel Cost: PKR 18,000 – 28,000 (4-day trip)

Malam Jabba

Summary: Famous ski resort surrounded by pine forests.

Major Attractions: Ski slopes, chairlifts, trekking routes.

Best Months: December – March (winter sports)

Culture & Food: Pashtun cuisine, lamb pulao, fried fish.

Travel Cost: PKR 12,000 – 20,000 (3-day trip)

Naran & Kaghan

Summary: Famous for lakes and green valleys, Naran & Kaghan attract thousands every summer.

Major Attractions: Saif-ul-Malook Lake, Lulusar Lake, Babusar Top.

Best Months: May – September

Culture & Food: Fresh trout, local tea houses, traditional Pashtun hospitality.

Travel Cost: PKR 20,000 – 35,000 (5-day trip)
